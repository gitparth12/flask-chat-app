Welcome to KeepItPrivate


KeepItPrivate is a place to chat with your friends, family, and colleagues. It's a simple and easy-to-use platform that allows you to connect with others and chat in real-time. How is it different from other platforms? Well it's very secure! While creating KeepItPrivate, we kept in mind our users' privacy and data security at every step of the process. With state of the art encryption and hashing algorithms, you can rest assured that your messages are always going to be private.

Features

  • Group Chatting: Start group chats with your friends or join existing chat rooms on a wide range of topics.
  • Real-time Messaging: Send messages to your friends and receive instant replies, no matter where you are.
  • File Sharing: Share photos, videos, and other files with your friends right from within the platform.
  • Custom Emojis: Use our custom emojis and stickers to add some fun to your chats.
